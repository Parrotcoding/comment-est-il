## Overview
The **American Revolutionary War** (1775‚Äì1783) was the conflict in which the Thirteen Colonies fought for independence from Great Britain. It resulted in the creation of the United States.

---

## Key Causes of the War

### Political Causes
- **Taxation without representation**
- British attempts to exert tighter control
- Restrictions on colonial self-government

### Economic Causes
- British taxes and acts:
  - Sugar Act  
  - Stamp Act  
  - Townshend Acts  
  - Tea Act  
- Trade restrictions under mercantilism

### Social Causes
- Enlightenment ideas (natural rights, liberty, self-rule)
- Rising colonial identity
- Tensions between colonists and British soldiers
